Exploring Accessibility Features in VR

I implemented a project that explores solutions to help visually impaired people navigate through a noisy ennvironment better. The development took place in Unity Game Engine and then software was deployed in a virtual reality environment. The application contains the accessibilty features built onto an obstacle avoidance procedure and series of procedures for the experiement. At the end, I conducted an experiment and collect data such as time take to travel and obstacle collisions to make appropriate conclusion about the helpfulness of accessibility feature.
